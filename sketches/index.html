Notes:
  • The hands point at 3:30 — could be morning or afternoon. The moon icon + "eftermiddag / kväll" disambiguates → correct answer is 15:30.
  • 14:30 is shown dimmed to demo the wrong-answer behavior. Kid keeps trying without losing the question.

Föräldraläge (Parent gate)

Math question when the gear is tapped · simple for parent, blocks 5-7yo

🏠
Förälder
⚙
Föräldraläge
7 + 6 = ?
Skriv svaret för att öppna inställningar.
Notes:
  • Numbers regenerate each time, range ~5+5 to 9+9 — too hard for a 5-7yo who hasn't learned two-digit math, easy for a parent.
  • Wrong answer → silently re-prompts with a new question. No retry counter, no lockout.
